The International Symposium on Low Power Electronics and Design (ISLPED) is holding the International Low Power Design Contest to provide a forum for universities to showcase original" power-aware" designs and to highlight the design innovations targeting power efficiency improvement. The goal is to encourage and highlight designoriented approaches for power reduction.
